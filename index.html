How I started!

My passion for coding started in highschool, although, I had been working on computers from a young age. After highschool I enrolled at St. Clair College in the Computer Systems Technology - Networking Program. After completing the Networking program I then took a year trying out jobs in the field. Although I enjoyed it, I enjoyed coding much more. I then decided to return to St. Clair to take the Internet Applications and Web Development program. There I learned more about different languages used to create web applications and became even more fascinated with the concept of creating web applications.
